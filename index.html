I received the M.S. degree from University of Southampton, Southampton, U.K., in 2020. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree from China University of Mining and Technology, Xuzhou, China, in 2019. My mission is to build interesting and user-friendly software and AI features for everyone. My research interests include multilabel classification, multimedia affective computing, multimedia aesthetics quality assessment and representation learning. Later, I focus on building trustworthy visual foundation models and MLLMs. Currently, I am a product manager focusing on software and AI products design. Before that, I worked as an AI algorithm engineer.
